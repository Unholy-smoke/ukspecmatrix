Competence ↓ 		Safe Practice Sustainability Ethics Inclusive Comms Critical Thinking
B1 Identify problems & opportunities Identify potential safety hazards early in the problem definition phase. Identify opportunities to reduce environmental impact before work begins. Identify ethical conflicts (e.g. dual use) in the project scope. Ensure diverse stakeholders define the "problem" together. Challenge whether the "problem" is the right one to solve.
B2 Design & Development Apply "Safety by Design" principles to eliminate risks. Choose materials/methods that lower lifecycle carbon. Refuse solutions that compromise integrity or public good. Present design concepts in accessible formats for all users. Evaluate multiple design options against evidence.
B3 Implementation Implement safe systems of work during deployment. Minimise waste and energy use during construction/coding. Report implementation issues transparently. Coordinate with teams clearly during rollout. Analyse implementation data to check performance vs theory.
C1 Plan & Manage Prioritise safety milestones in the project schedule. Include sustainability targets as project KPIs. Ensure contracts/plans are fair and legally compliant. Communicate plans to all levels of the organisation. Anticipate project risks and plan contingencies.
C2 Budgets & Resources Secure budget specifically for safety equipment/training. Allocate resources for sustainable alternatives (even if costlier). Prevent bribery and ensure supply chain transparency. Justify resource needs clearly to non-technical budget holders. Optimise resource usage to achieve maximum value.
C3 People Management Create a "psychologically safe" culture where staff can speak up. Train teams on sustainable practices. Treat all staff fairly and support their development. Actively support EDI and diverse hiring practices. Mentor staff to develop their own analytical skills.
C4 Quality & Improvement Review safety incidents to prevent recurrence. Measure carbon/waste data to drive future improvements. Adhere to QA standards and codes of conduct. Share lessons learned widely and clearly. Use feedback loops to refine processes systematically.
